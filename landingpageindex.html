Identity verification troubleshooting

This article provides solutions to common issues that may come up during the verification process.

Basics

  • Ensure your personal information matches exactly between your ID and Coinbase account, including your legal name, residence, date of birth, etc.
  • Even small discrepancies can cause a verification failure,so double check that all information is typed correctly on both your ID and profile.
  • Photos of your ID should be well-lit without glare, and with the entire document being visible. Carefully retake photos of your ID if it wasn't accepted.
  • Try an alternative upload method like the mobile app or using a diffrent web browser.
  • Wait the required time if receiving upload errors,support cant't bypass this restraction
